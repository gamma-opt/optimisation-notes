Consider the following integer programming problem $IP$:

\begin{align*}
	(IP) : \maxi z =~& x_1 + 2x_2   \\
	\st & -3x_1 + 4x_2 \le 4 \\
		& 3x_1 + 2x_2 \le 11 \\
		& 2x_1 - x_2 \le 5   \\
		& x_1, x_2 \in \integers_+
\end{align*}

Solve the problem by adding Gomory cuts to the LP relaxation until you find an integer solution.
